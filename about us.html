About Us

Phosphoruz Technologies

14+ YEARS OF EXPERIENCE

Phosphoruz Technologies is one of the leading digital marketing company centered in Thrissur, the cultural capital of Kerala, India.

Our enthusiastic experts are passionate about providing creative and cost-effective digital solutions to your problems. Starting from Web Designing to SEO and Social Media Management, we are here with you at every stage of your business.

We are a subsidiary of Oman based digital marketing agency, Pinky Bell. Launched into the tech world of the Sultanate of Oman in 2007, under the tag name of Pinky Bell Traders, we have been serving acclaimed clients of Oman and abroad since then. Phosphoruz Technologies endeavor to extend the same dedication and international expertise to cater to global services in the field of Information Technology and Communication to diverse business domains.

Today, we have a history of more than twelve years of proficiency and over 350 happy clients, which is still growing. Our expanding list of customers from well-distinguished business firms to start-ups attest to our company’s unwavering quality services that are at the same time affordable to all wallets. We make it our motto to bring you the best ever services that aid in the growth of your business.

Phosphoruz has rapidly attained a huge reputation among our clients for contributing mesmerizing designs and excellent contents, right from the inception. We always measure our company’s success by our customer’s contented smiles and work for your dreams with great passion. Our professionals would be working closely with you to take in your likes and suggestion to give you the best ever version.

We deliver all sorts of digital services including website development, portal development, web-based software development, intranet and extranet designing, e-commerce website development, search engine optimization (SEO), web and e-mail hosting, domain registration, cloud storage, online brand management, social media management (SMM), content writing, and anything you could think of. It is our business to take care of your business online.
